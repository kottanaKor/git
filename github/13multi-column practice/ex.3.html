Harmful properties of honey

With all the set of useful properties, honey has contraindications. First of all it concerns people with allergies to honey and its components. Sometimes it happens that only a specific variety causes an allergy honey. If you are in this category of allergies, then you do not need to completely abandon the honey, and just a selection method, you can choose the kind of food that will not be allergenic.

It is important to remember that heated over 40 degrees of honey loses all its useful properties, and heated over 60 degrees a degree also emits toxic oxymethylfurfural, which can cause serious harm organism.
